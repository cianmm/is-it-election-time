What should I do to get ready?

  • Ensure you are on the register. The deadline is Wed Jan 22 2020.
  • If you aren't on the register, get added to the supplemental register by filling in this form, having it witnessed at your local Garda station, and get it to your local County Council. The deadline is Wed Jan 22 2020.
  • If your address is wrong, update it on the supplemental register by filling in this form, having it witnessed at your local Garda station, and get it to your old address's County Council. The deadline is Wed Jan 22 2020.
