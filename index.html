Is It General Election Time Yet?

It will soon be General Election Time!

The General Election has been announced for the 29th of November.

Can I vote?

You can vote in a general election if you are:

  • An Irish or British citizen
  • Resident in Ireland
  • Aged 18 or over on the day of the election
  • Registered to vote

electoralcommission.ie

What should I do to prepare?

  • If you don't know who you're voting for, see which parties and candidates align with your values
  • Ensure you are on the register. Ensure that the address listed for you is somewhere that you will easily be able to get to on the 29th of November (so not home-home if you are not living at home). It is now past the deadline to register to vote.
